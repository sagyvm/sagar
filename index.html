Enterprise AI & Multi-Cloud Platform — MENAT Banking

Challenge

MENAT’s largest bank needed to adopt generative AI across development and production without compromising regulatory compliance, data security, auditability, or cost control across AWS and Azure estates.

Cloud Design & Implementation

Led design of an enterprise AI framework for governed consumption of AWS Bedrock, Azure AI Hub, and OpenAI models. Delivered a platform-as-a-product model with self-service provisioning, hybrid landing zones integrated with on-prem banking systems, cloud-native SDLC (microservices, GitOps, CI/CD), and automation at scale using Terraform, Jenkins, ArgoCD, and OpenShift across 30+ clusters in 8+ environments. Enforced AI governance, AWS Control Tower guardrails, and Azure Policy for cost optimization, fraud detection, and predictive analytics on OpenShift AI and Azure Cognitive Services.

Outcome

Established a centralized, audit-ready AI and cloud platform enabling secure multi-model access, faster compliant provisioning for product teams, and measurable governance and cost improvements across regulated banking workloads.

Digital Banking Transformation — Hybrid Kubernetes on AWS

Challenge

One of India’s largest banks required a cloud-native delivery model, zero-downtime migration of core lending platforms, and banking-grade security across hybrid production, non-production, and DR environments.

Cloud Design & Implementation

Spearheaded infrastructure modernization using DevOps and platform engineering principles. Architected hybrid Kubernetes hosting 80+ microservices across 20+ products; built self-service CI/CD with Jenkins shared libraries and YAML-driven pipeline configuration; migrated event-driven lending workloads (Kafka, MongoDB, EFK, web tier) to AWS with zero downtime. Implemented HashiCorp Vault and Consul with HSM integration, Istio multi-cluster service mesh with mTLS and canary releases, observability on EMR/EKS, and modular Terraform for EKS, MSK, ElastiCache, and databases with standardized Helm parent charts.

Outcome

Accelerated engineering agility with enterprise observability (Prometheus, Grafana, Jaeger, EFK, Alertmanager) while maintaining secure, repeatable deployments for mission-critical lending and payment workloads.
